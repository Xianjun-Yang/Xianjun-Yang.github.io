I am a Member of Technical Staff at [Reflection AI](https://reflection.ai), working on open intelligence for LLMs. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at University of California, Santa Barbara, where I was co-advised by Professor [Linda Petzold](https://engineering.ucsb.edu/people/linda-petzold), [William Yang Wang](https://sites.cs.ucsb.edu/~william/), and [Stephen Wilson](https://materials.ucsb.edu/people/faculty/stephen-wilson). Prior to joining Reflection AI, I worked at Meta Superintelligence Lab, where I work across FAIR, GenAI.

My recent research focuses on **Agentic RL** & **Mid-training** and previously I worked heavily on **AI Safety**, **LLM Security and Alignment**, **Mechanistic Interpretability**. I have published extensively at top-tier venues including ICML(Oral), NeurIPS, ICLR(Oral), and ACL, with around 3k citations.

Prior to AI, I quited my PhD in Fluid Mechanics when I leart AGI is coming. Then I earned my CS phd within 3 years without any prior cs background.
I received my B.S. in Energy and Power Engineering from Tsinghua University.
